Tea Recipe

Description:

Making a perfect cup of tea is simple yet satisfying. Whether you enjoy it with a splash of milk, a hint of lemon, or spiced with a touch of cinnamon and cardamom, tea is a timeless drink that fits any occasion. This recipe lets you brew a flavorful cup of tea to suit your taste, offering a great balance between rich flavors and a soothing experience. Whether you're starting your day or taking a break, tea is the perfect way to unwind and enjoy a moment of relaxation.

Ingredients:

  • Tea Leaves(or Tea Bags)
    • Black tea (e.g., Assam, Darjeeling, Earl Grey)
    • Green tea
    • Herbal tea (e.g., chamomile, peppermint, rooibos)
  • Water
  • Milk (optional)
  • Sugar(optional)
  • Spices
    • Cinnamon sticks
    • Cardamom pods
    • Cloves
    • Ginger (fresh or powdered)
    • Black pepper (optional for spicier chai)
  • Herbs
    • Mint leaves
    • Basil leaves
    • Lemon balm

Steps:

procedure (with milk):

  1. Boil the Water:
    • In a small saucepan or kettle, bring 1 cup of water to a boil.
  2. Add Tea Leaves:
    • Once the water is boiling, add 1 teaspoon of loose-leaf black tea (or a tea bag) to the boiling water
    • If you're making chai or spiced tea, add the spices (e.g., cardamom, cinnamon, ginger) to the water at this stage.
  3. Brew the Tea:
    • Let the tea leaves steep for 3-5 minutes. The longer it steeps, the stronger the tea will be. If you prefer a mild brew, steep for less time.
  4. Add Milk:
    • After the tea has steeped, add about 1/4 cup of milk to the brewed tea. You can adjust this amount depending on how creamy you want the tea to be.
    • Stir the tea to mix the milk well.
  5. Sweeten the Tea (Optional):
    • Add sugar or your preferred sweetener to taste. Stir until the sugar is dissolved.
  6. Strain the Tea (Optional):
    • If you used loose-leaf tea, strain the tea through a fine mesh strainer into your cup to remove the leaves. If you used a tea bag, simply remove it.
  7. Serve and Enjoy:
    • Your milky tea is ready! Serve hot, and enjoy it with your favorite snack or breakfast.
